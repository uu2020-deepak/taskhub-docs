    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about TaskHub.

    Account & Sign Up

    How do I create a TaskHub account?

    Visit taskhub.app and click "Sign Up". Enter your email, create a password, and verify your email address. Your account will be ready to use immediately.

    Can I use TaskHub without signing up?

    No, you need an account to use TaskHub. However, you can try the free version with limited features without a credit card.

    How do I reset my password?

    1. Go to the login page
    2. Click "Forgot Password?"
    3. Enter your email address
    4. Check your email for a reset link
    5. Click the link and create a new password

    Can I have multiple accounts?

    Yes, you can create multiple accounts with different email addresses. However, you cannot link them together.

    Tasks & Projects

    How many tasks can I create?

    Free plan: 100 tasks per month. Paid plans have unlimited tasks.

    What's the difference between a task and a project?

    A project is a container for organizing related tasks. A task is an individual item of work that needs to be completed.

    Can I move tasks between projects?

    Yes, use drag-and-drop or right-click on a task and select "Move to Project".

    How do I delete a task?

    1. Open the task
    2. Click the "..." menu
    3. Select "Delete"
    4. Confirm deletion

    Deleted tasks go to trash and can be recovered within 30 days.

    Can I duplicate a task?

    Yes, right-click a task and select "Duplicate". The new task will be created with the same properties.

    How do I set recurring tasks?

    1. Open a task
    2. Click "Set Recurrence"
    3. Choose frequency (Daily, Weekly, Monthly)
    4. Set an end date (optional)
    5. Save changes

    Collaboration

    How do I invite team members?

    1. Click "Team" in the sidebar
    2. Click "+ Invite Members"
    3. Enter email addresses
    4. Set their role (Member, Admin)
    5. Click "Send Invites"

    What are the different permission levels?

    • View: Can see tasks and projects but cannot make changes
    • Edit: Can create and modify tasks
    • Admin: Full control, can manage team settings and members

    Can I share a single task with someone?

    Yes, open the task and click "Share", then enter their email and select permission level.

    How do I remove a team member?

    1. Go to "Team Settings"
    2. Find the member
    3. Click the "..." menu
    4. Select "Remove"

    Notifications

    Why am I not receiving notifications?

    • Check your notification settings in preferences
    • Verify your email is confirmed
    • Check your spam folder
    • Ensure notifications are enabled in your browser

    Can I disable notifications?

    Yes, go to "Settings" → "Notifications" and toggle off notification types or select "Do Not Disturb" mode.

    How do I mute notifications for a specific project?

    Right-click the project and select "Mute Notifications". You can unmute anytime.

    Features & Functionality

    Can I attach files to tasks?

    Yes, click "+ Attach" in a task and select files from your computer, or drag and drop files directly.

    What file types can I attach?

    You can attach most common file types: PDF, Word, Excel, images, videos, and more. Maximum file size is 100MB.

    How do I search for tasks?

    Click the Search icon and type keywords. Results update in real-time. You can filter by status, assignee, or date.

    Can I create custom views?

    Yes, set your filters then click "Save View". Name your view and it appears in the sidebar for quick access.

    How do I export my data?

    1. Go to "Reports"
    2. Select a report
    3. Click "Export"
    4. Choose format (CSV, PDF, Excel)
    5. Click "Download"

    Billing & Plans

    What are the pricing plans?

    • Free: Up to 100 tasks/month, basic features
    • Pro: $9.99/month, unlimited tasks, advanced features
    • Team: $24.99/month, team collaboration, analytics

    Can I change my plan anytime?

    Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime from your "Billing" settings. Changes take effect immediately.

    Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

    Yes, annual plans include 20% discount compared to monthly billing.

    Can I cancel my subscription?

    Yes, go to "Billing" → "Subscription" and click "Cancel". Your account will downgrade to the free plan.

    Is there a free trial?

    Yes, try all Pro features free for 14 days. No credit card required.

    Technical Issues

    TaskHub is running slowly. What can I do?

    • Clear your browser cache
    • Disable browser extensions
    • Update your browser to the latest version
    • Check your internet connection speed
    • Restart the application

    I'm getting an error message. What should I do?

    1. Note the error code
    2. Refresh your browser or restart the app
    3. Check our Support Center
    4. Contact support with the error details

    Does TaskHub have offline mode?

    Desktop and mobile apps have limited offline functionality. Your changes sync automatically when reconnected.

    Is my data secure?

    Yes, TaskHub uses 256-bit encryption for all data in transit and at rest. We comply with GDPR and SOC 2 standards.

    Mobile & Desktop Apps

    Are there mobile apps?

    Yes, TaskHub is available on iOS and Android. Download from the App Store or Google Play Store.

    Can I sync across devices?

    Yes, all your data syncs automatically across web, desktop, and mobile apps.

    Does the desktop app require installation?

    Yes, download the installer from taskhub.app/download and follow the installation wizard.

    Can I use TaskHub offline?

    Desktop and mobile apps support offline mode. Changes sync when you reconnect to the internet.

    Integrations

    What integrations does TaskHub support?

    • Slack
    • Google Calendar
    • Microsoft Outlook
    • Webhooks for custom integrations

    How do I connect Slack?

    1. Go to "Settings" → "Integrations"
    2. Click "Connect Slack"
    3. Authorize the app
    4. Choose which notifications to send to Slack

    Can I integrate TaskHub with my own applications?

    Yes, TaskHub has a REST API and webhook support. See our API Reference.
